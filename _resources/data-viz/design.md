## 1. Know Your Audience
Tailor complexity and detail to your users.

## 2. Use Accurate Scales
Avoid misleading axes.

## 3. Make Comparisons Easy
Align data and use consistent scales.

## 4. Use Color Carefully
- Avoid too many colors
- Use accessible palettes
- Be consistent with color choices if using multiple plots

## 5. Reduce Clutter
Remove unnecessary elements.


## 6. Tell a Story
- Lead with conclusions
- Add annotations

---

## Key Takeaways
Good design improves understanding.
